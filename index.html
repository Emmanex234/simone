About Simone Susinna

Simone Susinna is an Italian actor and model, born on November 14, 1993, in Catania, Italy. He has made a significant impact in both the fashion and entertainment industries, transitioning seamlessly from modeling to acting with notable success.

Early Career

Susinna began his modeling career at the age of 18 after being scouted by Dolce and Gabbana. He quickly rose through the ranks, walking for prestigious brands like Emporio Armani and establishing himself as a sought-after model in the fashion industry.

Acting Breakthrough

Simone transitioned to acting with his breakout role in the Netflix movie "365 Days: This Day" (2022), where he played Marcello "Nacho" Matos. His performance garnered international attention and led to roles in the sequel "The Next 365 Days" and the film "Heaven in Hell".

Career Highlights

  • Featured in major fashion campaigns for Dolce & Gabbana and Emporio Armani
  • Breakout acting role in Netflix's "365 Days: This Day" (2022)
  • Reprised his role in the sequel "The Next 365 Days"
  • Starred in the film "Heaven in Hell"
  • Participated in the Italian reality show "L'Isola dei Famosi"
  • Represented by multiple modeling agencies internationally
